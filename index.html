The Dave Ramsey Snowball Method
This is the proven system. Follow the steps in order. Don't skip ahead.
1
List every debt — smallest to largest
Not by interest rate. By balance. The smallest debt gets attacked first regardless of rate.
2
Pay minimums on everything
Keep all other debts current. Never miss a minimum payment.
3
Attack the smallest debt with every extra dollar
Every single extra dollar goes at the smallest balance. Nothing else.
4
When it's paid — roll that payment into the next
Take the full amount you were paying and add it to the minimum of the next debt. The snowball grows.
5
Repeat until debt free
By the end you're throwing huge payments at your largest debt. The math becomes dramatic and the momentum is real.

Using on multiple devices
Your data lives on whichever device you're using. To use it on another device, export it here and import it there.
How to move to a new device
1. On your current device — tap Export My Data and save the file
2. Transfer that file to your new device (email it to yourself, AirDrop, etc.)
3. Open Clear Mind Co. on the new device — tap Import Data and select the file
4. All your goals, tasks, habits, streaks, and journal entries restore instantly
